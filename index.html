About Us

At Dad Can Fix It, LLC, we believe quality automotive repair starts with honesty, integrity, and treating every customer like family. Although our business officially opened in 2023, owner and lead technician Zach has been dedicated to the automotive industry since 2012.

Zach's journey began while he was still in high school, working at a small family-owned repair shop in Lampe, Missouri. That early experience sparked a passion for automotive repair and customer service that has guided his career ever since.

While attending Universal Technical Institute, Zach worked at a Ford Dealership in Irving, Texas, where he gained valuable dealership training and specialized experience. After graduation, he continued expanding his knowledge through fleet maintenance before returning to what he loved most- working in a small, family-owned repair shop where customers were treated as people instead of numbers.

After several years serving customers in Addison, Texas, Zach and his growing family relocated to Southwest Missouri in 2017. He spent several more years working at a local dealership before deciding to pursue his dream of owning his own shop and providing a more personal, honest approach to automotive repair.

Dad Can Fix It, LLC began as a mobile repair business, allowing Zach to build lasing relationships with customers througout the area. As demand continued to grow, he opened the shop's permanant location on Stonetree Drive in Branson, Missouri, where the business has proudly served the community since January 2025.

Today, Dad Can Fix It, LLC continues to provide dependable repairs at fair prices while treating every customer with the respect and honesty they deserve. Whether you need routine maintenance, brake service, suspension repairs, diagnostics, or heating and air conditioning repairs, we're committed to getting you safely back on the road.
